FAQ

1Why is my cake texture gummy? .

Air bubbles are essential for a cake to rise, but if your leavener is stale, the chemical reaction that causes the air bubbles to form will never happen, leaving your cake dense, gummy, and flat

2 Do you offer nut free products?

If there are nuts in any of our product, it is either presented visibly on the item or it is labeled with the type of nut in it. Our French Macaron shells are all made with almond flour.

3How do I leave feedback for an order?

We always like to hear from our customers. Please email us with your feedback.

4 How do I pay?

All payments are made online using our payment providers Stripe or PayPal. We also take cash on delivery. All major credit and debit cards are accepted.

5 Why order with My Baker?

Our network of talented bakers is selected to ensure each cake is hand-made freshly, from scratch, to order. This is why our cakes are still moist and delicious by the time they are delivered to your door.

6 What is shortening?

TBiscuits are made with “shortening“. The fat is rubbed into the flour and molecules of fat surround the flour particles and prevent water mixing with flour, which stops the development of gluten in the dough.

7 What makes a sucessful bakery?

Your customers are your key to success. Happy customers become repeat customers, so work to make each customer experience memorable
